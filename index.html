How does spaced repetition work? +
Spaced repetition is a learning technique that involves reviewing information at gradually increasing intervals. RecallForge schedules cards based on how well you know them - cards you find difficult appear more frequently than ones you know well.
Can I import existing flashcards? +
Yes! RecallForge supports importing flashcards from CSV files, Anki decks, and several other popular flashcard formats. Go to Decks > Import to get started.
How do I track my progress? +
The Stats section provides detailed analytics on your learning progress, including retention rates, study patterns, and predicted memory strength for different categories.
Is there a mobile app available? +
RecallForge is available on iOS and Android. Your account synchronizes across all devices automatically when signed in.
